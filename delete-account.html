Delete Account & Data

You have full control over your data. Follow the steps below to request deletion of your StepGenie account and associated data.

⚠️
This action is permanent and cannot be undone
Once your account and data are deleted, your coin balance, streak history, step records, and reward progress will be permanently lost and cannot be recovered.
📋 What Data is Deleted or Retained
Here's exactly what happens to each type of data when you submit a deletion request:
Google account info (name, email, profile)
Deleted
Step count & walking activity data
Deleted
Coin balance & streak history
Deleted
Device ID & push notification token
Deleted
Shipping address & phone number (pending orders)
Retained up to 90 days
App usage analytics
Anonymized
⏱️
All personal data is deleted within 15 business days of your verified request. Shipping data retained only if needed for active order fulfillment.
📧 How to Request Deletion
We don't have an automated in-app deletion button yet. Please follow these simple steps to submit your request:
1
Send an email to our support team
Email us at cwrprojects@gmail.com from the email address linked to your StepGenie account.
2
Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
This helps us identify and prioritize your request immediately.
3
Include your account details
In the email body, include your registered email address and confirm you want your account and all associated data permanently deleted.
4
We'll confirm and process your request
You'll receive a confirmation email within 2 business days. Your data will be fully deleted within 15 business days.
